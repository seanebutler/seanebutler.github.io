Use LibreOffice Impress to create a presentation for any occasion.

Steps

  1. Open the LibreOffice Suite package either by clicking on the icon on your desktop, or searching for "LibreOffice 4.3" in the start menu.

  2. Once the program opens, under Create, click Impress Presentation to open LibreOffice Impress. (Figure 13)

  3. Begin working on and adding slides to your slideshow. To add a new slide, right-click anywhere in the Slides area and select New Slide. (Figure 14)

  4. When finished editing your presentaion, or to ensure the safety of your presentaion, you need to save it. Do this by clicking on File on the top toolbar then click on Save As... (Figure 15)

    You should save your work periodically to avoid losing progress.


  5. Name your presentation and select where to save your it, then click Save.

  6. Either keep working on your presentation, or if you are finished, close the program by clicking the X at the top right of the window.
