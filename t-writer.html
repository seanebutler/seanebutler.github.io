Use LibreOffice Writer to create all sorts of documents and letters.

Steps

  1. Open the LibreOffice Suite package either by clicking on the icon on your desktop, or searching for "LibreOffice 4.3" in the start menu.
  2. Once the program opens, under Create, click Writer Document to open LibreOffice Writer. (Figure 7)

  3. Use the editor to type your letter. Use the tools at the top to format and change how your letter looks. (Figure 8)

  4. When finished editing, or to ensure the safety of your document, you need to save your document. Do this by clicking on File on the top toolbar then click on Save as... (Figure 9)

    You should save your work periodically to avoid losing progress.
